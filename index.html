My name is Riyasad Dhafeeq, a motivated and hardworking student currently in Grade 6 — English Medium — at AK-AL/Arafa Vidyalaya, Addalaichenai. From my very first day at KM/AK/Dr. Jalaldeen Vidyalaya, I have approached every lesson with curiosity and a genuine desire to grow.

My proudest academic milestone came when I successfully passed the Grade 5 Scholarship Examination in 2025 — earning both a Presidential Scholarship and entry into the English Medium stream. These achievements reflect years of disciplined preparation and consistent effort.

Beyond the classroom I am a competitive athlete who has represented my house and school with honour — achieving gold at the Kids Athletics Championship for consecutive years. I believe every facet of student life — academic, athletic, and personal — deserves the same unwavering commitment.
